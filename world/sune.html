Sune

Radoon's youngest daughter, also known as the Guardian of Love. Sune was created through the first kiss Radoon shared with his wife, Tymora. Tymora came late into Radoon's life and was essentially only Sune's mother. Both Radoon and Tymora loved Sune deeply, which is why she became the Goddess of Love, Peace, Life, Light, Beauty, and Passion. Sune is often represented as a creature holding many different shapes and forms in one being. Her round body and long blonde hair represent the softness that love and peace can bring. Her horns, tails, and different shaped limbs represent the equality between every being on Norrun, showing that everyone deserves love and light, and that beauty holds a different meaning for every culture.

Sune has many believers on Norrun. Every culture has its own interpretation of her. She may not be seen as a powerful deity by everyone, but she exists as a god in every culture. In large cities, temples are often built in her honor.

Tales are often told about her bringing people together in romance.
