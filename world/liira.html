Liira

Radoon's second child. Also known as Lady of Joy. It is told that Liira's creation occurred while Radoon was watching a sunset that shone on sheep clouds resting in the sky. He took one of the clouds and birthed Liira. They are the Goddess of Weather, Day and Night Cycle, Freedom and Joy. It is said that everyone has at least once met Liira when they had a joyous moment in their life. Any moment of joy that happened due to an interaction can most likely be traced back to Liira fooling around in the world of the living, disguising herself as one of them and living happily among them.

A children's tale says that if you ever feel down, you can wish for good company and Liira will appear to lighten your mood and grant your wish. Whether this tale is actually true remains up for debate.

In Ivia, Liira plays a central role in their beliefs, people pray to her for guidance through storms and to maintain their spirits.
